MoodShelf (Final Year Project)

MoodShelf is an iOS app designed to enable users to effectively manage their emotional health. By integrating features such as PHQ-9 and DASS-21 questionnaires for scientifically validated assessment, mood tracking and customizable emotion diaries, the application provides a comprehensive emotional health management solution. Nowadays, where mental health awareness is increasingly a priority, our app aim to train users to be more aware of their emotions.
